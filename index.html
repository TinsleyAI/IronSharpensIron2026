Heart over Status: A Father's True Identity

Most men live under the crushing weight of having to perform according to unspoken worldly pressures—such as financial status, material success, or continuous comparison.

Interactive Workshop Focus

We guide the room through an anonymous card exercise to demonstrate that the true requirements of fatherhood are not defined by pedigree or bank accounts. They are matters of character and heart, fully attainable by any man who surrenders to the Holy Spirit.

The Shield & The Shepherd

To reach unchurched men without immediate "churchy" friction, we translate the high-level biblical offices of King and Priest into practical, daily roles:

The Shield (King) Provision, rules, boundaries, and outer protection.
The Shepherd (Priest) Intimacy, emotional safety, mercy, and internal healing.

Jefferson Street), located just five minutes away from the Dismas facility.

II. Alignment with the Dismas Mission

Our summit’s theme, "Stronger Fathers, Stronger Families," is strategically engineered to align directly with Dismas Charities' foundational mandate: "Restoring Lives, Families, and Communities through successful Reentry."

Empirical data suggests that sustainable, long-term community reintegration relies heavily on the healing of family fractures and the restoration of parental accountability. Many returning citizens face extreme internal barriers regarding shame, familial guilt, and a lack of constructive templates for healthy fatherhood. This summit directly confronts these barriers, equipping men with practical, character-driven frameworks to rebuild trust and assume positive protective leadership roles within their households and neighborhood networks.

III. Curated Group Dynamics & Psychological Safety

We understand that traditional lecture-style events can feel threatening or institutional, causing participants to withdraw. To combat this, the summit utilizes advanced small-group facilitation techniques designed to foster an environment of absolute psychological safety and equality:

  • Round-Table Seating Configuration: Rather than sitting in isolated rows facing a stage, attendees are seated at collaborative round tables of 6–7 men. Status-symbols are completely removed; each table is anchored by a mature, vetted table facilitator whose primary directive is to model transparency and guide mutual sharing.
  • The "Shoulder-to-Shoulder" Methodology: To lower defensive barriers, the first hour focuses on common, low-stakes activities (a vehicle-themed icebreaker, physical worksheets, and anonymous card submissions) allowing men to bond naturally while working collaboratively, rather than putting them on the spot immediately.
  • The "Shield and Shepherd" Paradigm: This approach translates deep leadership concepts into highly relatable terms. We explore how a father must act as The Shield (providing structure, limits, rules, and outer safety) and The Shepherd (providing presence, active listening, forgiveness, and emotional safety). We highlight how over-indexing on rules without relationship breaks family trust, providing a balanced blueprint for domestic life.

IV. Logistical Accountability, Security & Transport

To ensure absolute compliance, programmatic alignment, and safety, Ministries of Christ assumes full accountability for the transport and monitoring of the participants:

  • Secure Transport: Chaperoned transportation will be coordinated by Ministries of Christ, picking up the resident participants directly from Dismas Lytle Street (1501 Lytle St) at 12:35 PM on June 20th, with flexible return coordination immediately following the conclusion of the event, including any personalized ministry or prayer time.
  • Proximity: The transit distance is approximately 0.8 miles (a brief 5-minute drive), drastically reducing transit risks.
  • Accountability Checks: The designated drivers and table hosts will maintain roster accountability throughout the duration of the 3-hour event.
  • Full Provision: Full buffet-style catering is provided by Swanie's Catering. The event, transportation, and dining are offered completely free of charge to Dismas Charities and its residents.

We appreciate your partnership in the LOVE Program and hope to extend that same transformational impact to these gentlemen on June 20th. Thank you for your leadership, dedication, and consideration of this proposal.
